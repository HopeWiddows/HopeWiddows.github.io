Welcome!

Greetings! My name is Hope, and I am a student developer. Thank you for visiting my page!


My most recent professional experience was in Social Services, where I worked as a Residential Youth Worker. It was my dream job, and despite the challenges and heartbreak, I was motivated every day by the incredible and often misunderstood young people I had the privilege to work with.


However, life has its twists and turns. A diagnosis of a chronic illness required me to step back from youth work, giving me time to reflect and consider a new direction. After much contemplation and many conversations, I realized that I had a genuine interest in understanding the intricacies of technology, sparked by my fascination with Stardew Valley mods.


A pivotal conversation with a friend who transitioned from the art world to the tech industry and found great fulfillment confirmed my new path. I eagerly researched various educational opportunities, from degrees to bootcamps, and soon I was enrolled and on my way to a new dream career.


Learning to code has been an exhilarating journey. While it comes with its fair share of challenges, I find immense satisfaction in the continuous learning and growth it demands. I am excited about my future in this dynamic field and look forward to the constant evolution it promises.
